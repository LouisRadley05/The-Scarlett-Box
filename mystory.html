My Story

Scarlett Barnett-Smith
Founder & CEO

Hello, I’m Scarlett — a maritime professional and the founder of The Scarlett Box. Working at sea for several years, I witnessed firsthand the lack of access to menstrual products and support available to women onboard ships.

What began as frustration evolved into a mission: to ensure that every seafarer, regardless of gender or location, has access to the dignity and comfort they deserve. The Scarlett Box was born out of necessity — and built with compassion.

The Scarlett Box is designed to provide practical, discreet, and reliable menstrual care solutions for crew members at sea. Every element of the box has been thoughtfully created to meet the challenges of life offshore — compact, sustainable, and easy to distribute.

Today, The Scarlett Box is more than a product — it’s a movement for equality, dignity, and awareness in the maritime industry. Thank you for joining me on this journey.
