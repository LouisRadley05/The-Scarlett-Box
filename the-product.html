What the Product Is & Where It’s Used

Image Placeholder (e.g., The Scarlett Box installed on a vessel)

The Scarlett Box is the flagship product of The Scarlett Box LTD — an emergency period care solution that provides comfort, dignity, and preparedness for female seafarers.

It is designed specifically for the maritime environment, ensuring essential menstrual products are always available where they’re needed most: in the workplace restroom.

Life at sea involves long hours, harsh conditions, and high-pressure environments where emergencies must be planned for in advance. However, access to menstrual products is an emergency that has been historically overlooked. There is currently no standard industry provision for such situations, leaving a gap that affects comfort, dignity, health, safety, and performance.

The Scarlett Box addresses this gap with a compact, hygienic, fully equipped menstrual care solution tailored for women working on board ships and in maritime settings.

Dimensions & Type of Box

Box Dimensions: 225 x 275 x 90 mm

The Scarlett Box is designed as a first-aid–style emergency menstrual care box that can be placed in workplace toilets, especially in areas women cannot leave due to duty requirements.

Why This Type of Box Is Needed

  • Provides essential menstrual care items in a compact, hygienic format
  • Ensures readiness for an often-unplanned and underserved emergency
  • Supports comfort, dignity, and well-being
  • Offers a low-cost, high-impact solution for a male-dominated industry
  • Helps shipping companies and ports create more inclusive, supportive environments for women
  • Aligns with ongoing efforts to promote and retain women in maritime roles
