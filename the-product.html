The Scarlett Box

The Scarlett Box is a first-of-its-kind emergency menstrual health kit designed for women working in maritime. It ensures that menstrual health never stands in the way of safety, performance, or dignity — wherever duty takes you.

The Box

Designed for onboard use, The Scarlett Box is compact, durable, and easily mountable. It includes all essential items for immediate menstrual support — ready when needed most. Dimensions, placement options, and custom branding are available upon request.

What's Inside?

Each Scarlett Box includes essential menstrual products, hygiene items, and a contents card to ensure replenishment is simple. Our key supplier, LiceTtes, provides eco-friendly and high-quality products trusted by seafarers worldwide.
