The Scarlett Box Product

The Scarlett Box is a practical and discreet menstrual care solution designed specifically for women in the maritime industry. It provides comfort, preparedness, and dignity for female seafarers working in remote or male-dominated environments.

Each box is stocked with essential sanitary items, educational resources, and practical hygiene products, offering a low-cost, high-impact solution that supports inclusion and wellbeing at sea.
