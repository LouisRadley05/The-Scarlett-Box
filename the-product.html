What the Product Is & Where It’s Used

Image Placeholder (e.g., The Scarlett Box in a maritime restroom)

The Scarlett Box is the flagship product of The Scarlett Box LTD — an emergency period care solution that provides comfort, dignity, and preparedness for female seafarers.

Designed specifically for the maritime environment, it ensures essential products are always available where they’re needed most: in the workplace restroom.

Life at sea involves long hours, harsh conditions, and high-pressure environments where emergencies must be planned for in advance. However, access to menstrual products is a recurring emergency that has historically been overlooked.

There is currently no standard provision for such situations throughout the maritime sector. The Scarlett Box addresses this gap with a compact, hygienic, fully equipped menstrual care solution for women working on board ships and in maritime settings.

Period Product Supply – Lil-Lets

Image Placeholder (Lil-Lets product range)

All period products provided inside The Scarlett Box will be supplied by Lil-Lets, a trusted and well-established brand known for developing high-quality menstrual products designed with comfort and performance in mind.

Lil-Lets is recognised for its commitment to understanding the needs of people who menstruate, offering a wide range of products including tampons, pads, liners, and menstrual health essentials. Their focus on comfort, absorbency, and user-centred design aligns with The Scarlett Box’s mission to ensure dignity and preparedness for women in the maritime industry.
