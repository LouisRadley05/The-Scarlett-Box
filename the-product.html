The Scarlett Box

The Scarlett Box is an emergency menstrual health kit designed for women in maritime, providing essential supplies where access to products may be limited. Its purpose is to promote dignity and inclusivity within the maritime industry.

The Box

The box itself is durable, compact, and easily placed in workplace toilets or key locations on board ships. It ensures access to menstrual products at all times. Dimensions and layout are designed for convenience and hygiene.

What's Inside?

Each Scarlett Box includes a range of essential products such as pads, tampons, wipes, disposal bags, and hygiene items. We also partner with sustainable suppliers like Licettes to ensure environmental responsibility.
