Welcome to The Scarlett Box

The Scarlett Box is our flagship product — an emergency menstrual products box designed to provide comfort, dignity, and preparedness for female seafarers, available right where they need it most: the workplace restroom.

The Scarlett Box Ltd is a UK-based company founded by Scarlett Barnett-Smith, a Deck Officer with nearly a decade of experience in the maritime industry.
